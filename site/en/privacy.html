Hosting and delivery

The public website is provided via GitHub Pages. For externally reachable requests, a tunnel connection via Cloudflare may additionally be active.

Accessing the website creates technically necessary connection data. The legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR.

Request form

The request form processes name, email address, phone number, requested service, device, preferred contact path and issue description.

The legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR.

Local storage

Incoming requests are stored in a local SQLite database within the internal request management and used only for handling the case.

Phone and WhatsApp contact

If you contact Pixel & Parts by phone or WhatsApp, the transmitted data is used only to answer or process your request.

Storage period

Requests and contact data are stored only for as long as required for processing, follow-up communication, any resulting order or legal obligations.
